Our Hotel Services

  • 1.

  • Room service: Guests are allowed to order food and beverages to their room. Room service is available 24/7, and there may be an additional charge for this service.

  • 2.

  • Concierge: The hotel concierge can assist guests with various tasks such as making restaurant reservations, arranging transportation, or providing information about local attractions.

  • 3.

  • Housekeeping: Our housekeeping service involves cleaning and tidying guests rooms, including making the bed, replenishing towels and toiletries, and vacuuming or sweeping the floors.

  • 4.

  • Fitness center:Guests can use the fitness center to maintain their fitness routine while traveling, without having to leave the hotel premises. Our fitness centers are open 24/7 or during specific hours, depending on the hotel's policy. It usually includes a range of equipment such as cardio machines, weight machines, and free weights.

  • 5.

  • SPA:Guests can also choose from a variety of facial and body treatments that are designed to cleanse, exfoliate, and hydrate the skin. Our spa services are provided by licensed professionals who have undergone extensive training and have expertise in various techniques and modalities. A range of massages, such as Swedish, deep tissue, or hot stone massages are provided.
